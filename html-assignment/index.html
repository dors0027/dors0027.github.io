About Me

Hi, my name's Kevyn, a 19 year old college student at Algonquin College. I'm also the 2nd child of 4, with 2 brothers and a sister. I'm of Haitian descent and also have family ties in France, as that is where my father grew up. As you can probably guess, I speak French, which is in fact my 1st language. What you probably didn't guess is that I'm actually trilingual, speaking French, English and Creole.

One of my biggest passions is football. European football. I've been playing since I was a toddler, and always dreamed of playing in the UEFA Champions League. Growing up, my favorite player was Neymar. His way of dribbling, controlling and kicking the ball was nothing short of amazing to me. I remember him saying:

A historic defeat cannot bow our heads forever. -Neymar Jr.
I feel a lot of people can relate to this because when life pushes you down, you must push through life and always keep your head up no matter what.
