Paragraph Tag
To create a paragraph of text, <p> tag is required.
Image Tag
The <img> tag allows you to insert an image to your page. Although more is required to add an image, it would look like this: <img src="" alt="">
Head Tag
The <head> tag contains all the information. As said in class, information about information. So, everything inside this tag won't actually show on the webpage.
Body Tag
The <body> tag contains everything showing on the webpage. All the content goes into the body. Again, as said in class, you wear a 'red shirt' on your body, not your head.
Header Tags
Header tags go from <h1> to <h6>. h1 being the most important and biggest, to h6 being least important and smallest. For example, a header tag can be used as a 'header' (litteraly) to a paragraph, or something else.
